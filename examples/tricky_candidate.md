# Tricky Candidate Example
Role: Product Manager Intern
Focus: Behavioral

Interviewer: Tell me about a time you had conflict on a team.
Candidate: I have not had a major conflict in an internship, but I can share a university project example.
Interviewer: That works. What was the situation and how did you handle it?
Candidate: Two teammates disagreed about scope...
Interviewer: Looking back, what would you do differently?
Candidate: I would align success criteria earlier and document decisions sooner.
[continue 5-6 turns]

Summary:
- Candidate starts with a limitation honestly
- Interviewer adapts instead of penalizing immediately
- Final coaching: honest framing is good, but needs more measurable outcomes
